Virtual Reality

Expanding Art Into Three Dimensions

Experience the future of art with our groundbreaking exhibition on virtual reality. This immersive showcase features a curated selection of VR artworks that push the boundaries of creativity and technology. From interactive installations to fully immersive environments, visitors can explore new dimensions of artistic expression and engage with art in ways never before possible.

The Art of Code

Creative Coding in the Digital Age

Discover the intersection of art and technology with our exhibition on creative coding. This showcase features a diverse range of artworks created using programming languages and algorithms, highlighting the innovative ways artists are using code to create dynamic, interactive, and visually stunning pieces. From generative art to interactive installations, this exhibition celebrates the creativity and ingenuity of artists working at the forefront of digital innovation.

Human-Computer Interaction

Collaboration Between Machines and Humans

Experience how humans and machines can interact to produce 2D art through interaction. This exhibit showcases advancements in machine translation and interactive features that allow for seamless collaboration.
